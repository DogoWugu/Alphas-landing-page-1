Recommended Wallets for ALPHAS Token

ALPHAS is an ERC20 token, residing on the Ethereum blockchain. To safely store and manage your ALPHAS tokens, it's crucial to use compatible wallets. Here's our top recommendation:

  • Metamask: A popular browser-based wallet that's easy to use. It integrates with most web3 platforms seamlessly.

How to install MetaMask:

  1. Visit metamask.io
  2. Hit "Download" in the menu bar.
  3. Click “Install MetaMask for Chrome”. You will be directed to the Chrome Web Store.
  4. Install MetaMask Chrome
  5. Click “Add to Chrome”.
  6. On the pop up, click “Add extension”.

  7. After adding MetaMask Extension, MetaMask will automatically open. You can also make sure it's easily accessible in your toolbar by clicking the jigsaw icon in the top-right of the screen, and hitting the pin icon.

Firstly: do not download MetaMask from anywhere other than the official app store appropriate to your device/OS. Cloned versions of MetaMask are out there that are programmed to record your Secret Recovery Phrase as soon as it's generated. To avoid falling victim to these scams, make sure you download the official MetaMask app from the app store on your device, and not from any other source.

  1. Open the App Store and hit the search button in the bottom right. Input MetaMask into the search bar.

  2. Install MetaMask iOS

    Either click 'Get' or tap on the name of the app to see more details. Complete the usual App Store download confirmation process.

  3. Once installed, open the app and follow the prompts to create your wallet.
