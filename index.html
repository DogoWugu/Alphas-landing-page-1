What is the tax used for?

When you purchase ALPHAS tokens, 1% supports marketing while another 1% aids global elephant charities. If you decide to sell, 1% supports marketing, another 1% enters you into a token holder lottery, and the final 1% is burned to ensure our token's value remains strong.

Supply Breakdown

  • 20% is set aside for other exchanges.
  • 10% gets burned to keep the token valuable.
  • 15% is for those who stake their tokens and earn rewards.

    FAQ

    What is $ALPHAS Token?

    $ALPHAS Token is a cryptocurrency project that is community-driven with an aim to benefit all its participants. The initiative is aimed at bringing change to the crypto narrative by offering transparency and a community-focused approach.

    Who is the founder of $ALPHAS Token?

    $ALPHAS Token was founded by Andrew Fisher, a visionary from a small town in Iowa. His journey, from humble beginnings to becoming a cryptocurrency visionary, has played a significant role in shaping the vision for ALPHAS Token.

    What are the fees?

    For every purchase, 1% goes to charity and 1% to marketing. On sales, 1% is designated for marketing, 1% for a lottery, and 1% goes to burn.
