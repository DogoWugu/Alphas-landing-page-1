The ALPHA's Monthly Lottery

The Alphas and I were driven by a simple yet powerful vision: to give every ALPHA holder, regardless of the size of their holdings, a chance to grow their portfolio. In the vast crypto universe, it often feels like only the whales reap the rewards, while the small fishes, those holding modest amounts yet displaying unwavering loyalty, get overshadowed.

This is why we launched our monthly lottery initiative. A slice of our sell tax directly flows into a dedicated lottery wallet. Every month, we randomly select a holder to receive these accumulated rewards. The beauty of this system? Any ALPHA holder stands a chance to win, irrespective of the size of their wallet.

One pivotal rule: loyalty is key. If a selected wallet has made a recent sale, it's passed over, and another lucky holder gets the spotlight.

Hold on, believe in the vision, and you might just be our next winner. Good Luck Fellow Alphas!
